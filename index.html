    Welcome to the Equity Analytics Support Site #

    The Why Behind Equity Analytics #

    Equity Analytics was created to offer a scalable, data-driven approact to create customizable data trackers and reports for school district leaders. Unlike most programs, Equity Analytics creates reports based on the needs of the district, schools, and most importantly – teachers.

    Everything that Equity Analytics offers has been created with the intention to save time for district leaders, school leaders, and teachers so that they can use this extra time to focus on what maatters most – educating the students of Mississippi. Please work!

    To that same notion, if you ever find yourself thinking that there could be an improvement made to Equity Analytics, please do not hesitate to email the founder directly. Equity Analtyics is not a “one-size fits all” solution – we pride ourselves on being a easy-to-use resource – unlike certain websites and programs like SamSpectra, MSIS, and TIMS.

    Ten Minute Rule

    We ask that you try to use this help site to answer any questions you may have, but if find yourself spending more than ten minutes and still cannot find your answer, email the Help Desk and we will respond as quickly as we can.

    About the Founder #

    The idea that led to Equity Analytics started in Room 12 of the Inverness Elementary School located in Inverness, MS. I was was frustrated with how much data I had access to but no good way to quickly analyze and make decesions with the data. Especially my becnhmark data, I had no good way to figure out how my students were performing on their benchmark relative to the Mississippi Department of Education (MDE) accountability model. Fastword to today, Equity Analytics is centered around the idea of making the lives of educators easier by have a “one-stop shop” for all thier student’s data.
